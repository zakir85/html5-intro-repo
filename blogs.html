Travel to Nepal

Published on: 16 June 2023

Overview

Visit Kathmandu, Pokhara and Nagarkot in this 6 nights and 7 days tour with ZTA. Get to see the Himalayan vibe and magnificent view with sightseeing around multiple spots. So, pack your bags and get out to explore the adventure. Great opportunity to experience Temple, Heritage sites, Monuments & monasteries, natural beauty & mesmerizing view of mountains. The most visited cities are Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, Pokhara, Chitwan & Lumbini along with a few hill stations.

Highlights:

Best Month for Travel: February, March, April, May, June, September, October, November, December. Nepal is very suitable throughout the year for city tours. However, we have avoided 3 months due to raining & heavy cold.

How to go to Nepal from Bangladesh:

You can travel to Nepal by taking a direct flight Dhaka to Kathmandu . However, If you want to come through land then there are different land borders to go to Nepal. The best way to come to Nepal is through the Raxaul border. Please connect with us for more info.
